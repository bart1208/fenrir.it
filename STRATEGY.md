This site is not just a personal portfolio; it's a **Digital Declaration of Independence**. It represents the intersection between a lifelong passion for building (from the 386 era to AI orchestration) and a philosophical outlook on the future of humanity (The Singularity).

## Core Pillars

### 1. The Forge (La Fucina)
*   **The Soul:** Technical expertise, "getting hands dirty" with code and hardware.
*   **Content:** Deep integration with GitHub. Showcasing real projects like "L800 Pizzeria" and "L'Apprendista del Branco".
*   **Goal:** To prove competence beyond corporate boundaries (moving away from the betting industry towards value-driven projects).

### 2. The Observatory (L'Osservatorio)
*   **The Soul:** Philosophical and critical thinking.
*   **Content:** Essays and thoughts on the Singularity, the limits of biological life, AI ethics, and the role of humans as "intent-givers".
*   **Goal:** To leave a legacy of values and vision for the next generation (Bart's children).

### 3. The Nomad (Il Nomade)
*   **The Soul:** Agnostic geography. Seville is a coordinate, but the world (and the universe) is the workspace.
*   **Content:** Minimalist, clean, and futuristic design. 
*   **Goal:** To position Bart as a global professional, ready for a future where work is optional and driven by shared beliefs.
